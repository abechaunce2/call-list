Call Recording — Test

This checks whether your work computer will let the browser record both sides of a Salesforce call. Takes 1 minute. Nothing is sent anywhere — a test recording only downloads to your computer. Use Chrome or Edge.

Step 1 — turn on capture

Click the button. Windows pops up a share box → pick Entire Screen (or your Salesforce tab) → check the “Also share system audio” / “Share tab audio” box (bottom-left) → click Share.

Step 2 — do both bars move?

🟢 YOU (your microphone)
🔵 THEM (the call audio)

Talk out loud → the green bar should move. Play any video/music on the computer (or make a test call) → the blue bar should move. If both move, recording will capture both sides.
